Cebu is famously known as the "Queen City of the South" and is the oldest city and first capital of the Philippines. As the "cradle of Christianity" in the region, it is a premier tourist destination, top economic, industrial, and educational hub, and is celebrated for its rich history, vibrant Sinulog festival, and, to some, as the "Creative Capital" of the country.
